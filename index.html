Easily manage your prayer requests, with Prayer Vault!

Prayer Vault is a new, easy to use app which stores all your prayer requests for easy access. With over 20 categories and support for contacts, notes, and custom information, Prayer Vault will keep your prayer requests organized. No more excuses for forgetting a request. In seconds you can have it up and running.

Prayer Vault is $2.00 in the United States. There is no lite version at this time. Check out the screenshots to get a feel for the app, or you can shoot us an email at prayervault@gmail.com for more information.

Features

  • Organize Your Prayer Requests
  • Import Your Contacts
  • Quick Access to Your Prayers
  • Intuitive Interface
  • High Flexibility
