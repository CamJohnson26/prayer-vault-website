Prayer Vault Released: Brings Prayer Request Tracking to iPhone / iPad

Little Rock, AR    Apr 19, 2013 in Lifestyle

 

Today Cameron Johnson releases version 1.0.0 of Prayer Vault, bringing prayer tracking to iPhones and iPads. With a stunning interface and intuitive features, Prayer Vault fills a huge void in the Christian app market, providing a clean, beautiful way to organize prayer requests.

 

[prMac.com] Rancho Mirage, CA - Rancho Mirage, California — February 3, 2012 — Today Cameron Johnson releases version 1.0.0 of Prayer Vault, an app for iPhone and iPad that can organize prayer requests. Notable features include the ability to import contacts and a clean, intuitive interface.

Although brand new, Prayer Vault fills a much needed void in the Christian app market. Unlike many prayer tracking apps available in the app store, Prayer Vault focuses on simplicity of features and maintains a clean, elegant interface.

Prayer Vault is $1.99 USD, and is available for all iOS devices with iOS 5.0 and above. Users can add prayer requests for anyone in their contacts and have the ability to add notes, dates, and titles and can also choose between 20 categories. Once entered, prayers can be sorted by category, person, or date, and can be deleted or marked as resolved. A contact can be selected and communicated with directly from within the app.

Prayer Vault focuses on graphics and simplicity. It is useful for anyone trying to organize their prayer life, but is specifically designed for the Christian market. Depending on user interest, new features will be added in the future including password protection and local reminders of upcoming prayers.
